# Ash Collector

The Ash Collector (AC) is an NPC at **141,Hell,174** who spends the ash your character collects (check your total with the /ash command). You must travel to his location to use him, and because he stands on Hell magma you need all planes access, Templar status, and a Templar's wand — Surface-bound players cannot use him. Do not confuse him with his relative the Ash Keeper at 150,Sur,150. The AC has two services: summoning beasts (public or private) and attaching devoids to D*D relics.

## Summoning Beasts

Public beasts cost less ash but trigger a yellow system message to every player announcing the spawn location — it shows even with all chat types disabled. Anyone can race to the spot and kill the beast, so the summoner who paid may not get the kills.

Public beasts come in two flavors:

- **Guaranteed public:** 1.5 bil gold and 2,250 ash. A beast will definitely spawn, but the type and location are random and there is no guarantee you get the kill.
- **Not guaranteed public:** 1 bil gold and 1,000 ash. The cheapest option, but it fails roughly half the time — on a failure the AC mocks you and nothing spawns; on a success a random beast appears at a random location.

Private beasts cost far more ash (7,000–8,500 vs. 1,000 or 2,250 for public), but the location is shown only to the summoner. You get to fight the beast alone with no one sniping it, which makes privates the only realistic option for players who need a long time to kill a beast.

To summon: visit the AC and give him gold. He deducts the required ash and summons the beast. The gold amount selects the beast type — give the wrong amount and you get the wrong beast, with **no refunds** for mistakes. The 2 bil gold summon (7,500 ash) yields one of three possible beasts and **you do not get to choose which**. All three give an approbation, but if the one that appears is a beast you already killed that monty, you will **not** be able to fight it. Use the /beast command to see which beasts you have already killed this monty.

## Devoid Relics

The AC can add a devoid to a D*D relic — a Devil's*Deliverance relic where * is Mutilating, Lithe, Putrid, Resistant, Skillful, Accelerated, Warring, or Opulent.

- Cost: 1,000 ash plus one pure devoid.
- Procedure: bring the D*D relic, a pure devoid, and the ash to 141,Hell,174 and trade the AC the D*D relic. He takes the devoid and 1k ash and hands back a D*Devoid (D*Dv) relic.

## Ash Collector Dialog

The AC has little to say. Messaging him a greeting gets a dismissive reply that points you at the bracketed keyword **collecting**; following the keyword chain collecting → summon → public → private walks through his price list: 1 bil gold + 1,000 ash for a possible summon, 1.5 bil + 2,250 ash for a risk-free public beast, 2 bil + 7,500 ash for a private approbation-yielding beast, and for slightly under 2 bil plus a chunk of ash he offers the specific beast types — offer an amount and he tells you what he can do.
