# Special Locations

## Overview

Most of the game's 450,000 kingdoms are identical: the same list of 70 creatures, with the same stats under different names as you change planes. A handful of kingdoms — usually called "special kingdoms" — break that pattern with harder enemies, more gold, better skilling odds, higher drop rates, or quest roles. This page consolidates all of them.

Plane-wide bonuses (relative to Surface):

| Plane | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Dungeon | 2x Surface gold |
| Sky | 2x Surface experience |
| Hell | 3x Surface experience |
| Heaven | 3x Surface gold |

## How Drops Work at These Locations

- Some items drop or convert automatically the moment you enter the location.
- Other items require killing creatures from the New Fight list.
- If a specific creature (or range) is named, fight and kill those.
- If no specific creature is mentioned, kill anything you can and keep killing until the item drops or transforms.
