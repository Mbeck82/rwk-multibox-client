# Quest Walkthroughs

## Overview

RWK quests grant access to more locations, more killing power, higher crafting caps, faster leveling, and more. Most require paid upgrades, but ash can substitute for cash on nearly all of them, so enough play time can complete every quest for free.

Key rules that apply to all quests:

- Quest items appear in your inventory under **Others**. They cannot be traded, sold, or destroyed. Once you have one, it stays until a combinational quest rolls several items into one.
- Purchasing an item from Glitchless (the game's Buy Items button) costs real US dollars, not game gold.
- If a quest requires a location and the item does not drop on entry, kill any creature there until it drops. When a specific creature is required, it is named in the tables below.
- Multi-step quests are listed in the order the steps must be performed. Prerequisites are items you must already possess.

Access requirements referenced throughout (the source color-codes these):

- **Sky access** quests need a Mountain Tamer ($50 purchase or 50,000 ash).
- **Dungeon access** quests need a Lava Splicer ($50 purchase or 50,000 ash).
- Some quests need **both Sky and Dungeon** access, or all-planes access.
- **All planes** quests need a Transcendental Sanction, Omega Sanction, Sanction of Surpassment, or Sanction of Serenity.
- **Water** quests need Aqua Lungs ($20 purchase or 20,000 ash) or an Omega Sanction to enter Surface water kingdoms.
