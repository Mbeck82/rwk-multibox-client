# Enchant Crafting

## Overview

Chantcraft is the crafting skill for enchanting and disenchanting — adding or removing relics from spells, armor, weapons, bows and arrows. Chanting uses both the chantcraft and rune working skills. The skill level required is the same whether you enchant or disenchant, and it depends on the **base value of the item**, not the relic — all relics are equal for chanting purposes. Base value is the item's store price (Actions, Buy menu): a Rusty Dagger has a base value of 10 gold, a Rustless Lifeceaser 1 billion gold. The highest-quality items are not sold in the store; they are player-crafted or beast drops.

Chantcraft is capped at **10** without a Tradeskill Manifesto (TSM) and at **999** with one. No item is trivial to chant at skill 10, so without a TSM you always have a chance of failure, even on the cheapest items. The TSM is a quest item.

Skill values for items up to 1 bil base come from Cyril's site; higher values are extrapolated but match observed training.

## Requirements

- You must be **king of a kingdom**, and that kingdom must hold at least 1 rune.
- Runes are consumed on every attempt, success or fail. The item and relic are never at risk.
- Cheap items use 1 rune per attempt; above a base-value threshold this rises to 2 runes.
- Demonic, earthen and crystalline items (the 3 highest quality classes) use **250 runes per attempt**, succeed or fail. High-level chanting gets very expensive very fast.

## How It's Done

- Open the Actions menu, choose Enchant or Disenchant, select the item (and relic for enchanting), then Submit.
- Success when enchanting combines the item and relic; success when disenchanting splits them into the pure item and a pure relic.
- Failure costs only the rune(s) — nothing else happens.
- You cannot enchant onto an item that already has a relic. To swap relics you must disenchant first, then re-enchant — a minimum of 2 runes (2 bil gold), so swapping relics on cheap items or low-value relics is rarely cost effective.

## Getting Started Cheaply

Disenchanting also trains chantcraft, and you can recoup costs by selling pure relics to the store for 1 bil each.

- Collect low-base-value drop items carrying relics that store-sell for 1 bil: **Cara**, **Death Spike**, **Blood Bath**, **Vamp Leech**, **Stream Of Conscious**, **Dex Hoist**. Each successful disenchant returns the 1 bil rune cost when you sell the relic.
- Alternatively, craft (buying is less cost-efficient) 5 of the 20k-base relics — **Confusion**, **Life Roulette**, **Annulment**, **Damage Shield**, **Damage Capacitor** — then disenchant items carrying that same relic. Once you hold 6 pure relics of one type, the first sells back to the store for 1 bil, recouping the cost.
- This works until your skill requires 2 runes per chant; after that a success only returns half your cost. A mule helps for stockpiling items so you can disenchant many with the same relic in one session off the same 5 primer relics.

## Chanting Normal Items

Normal items have no prefix (Rusty Dagger, Timeless Fortitude helmet, Meteor Swarm spell). **Skill Available** is the minimum skill to attempt the item; at **Skill Trivial** you never fail but also gain no further skill — progress requires more expensive items.
