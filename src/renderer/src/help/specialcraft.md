# Special Crafting

Craftable "special" item lines: durability armor, special weapons (light, heavy, or precise), special spells (rapid, major, accurate), and bows/arrows (which have no special versions). Most pre-Demonic values come from Cyril's site (now defunct), used with permission. Page last updated 9 March 2025.

Items at the top of the skill range (Demonics, Superior Treasures) appear to only become trivial by killing Grandmaster Templars — crafting alone never seems to trigger the last few points. Great Treasures behaved the same way before Superior ones existed.

## Forged Items

**These items can NOT be traded in any form, and owning one prevents name swaps.** While a forging is in progress, that Forge kingdom gains no interest and produces no gems; other Forge locations are unaffected.

- Made by upgrading a **PURE** Shadow Scale item in a Forge kingdom using gold and Greater Gems; requires a SoMC.
- Initial cost: 10 mil Forge Trs (10 trillion normal gold), 5 Greater Gems, 5 days to make. Cost rises for each additional item (table below). A mix of different Great Gems can be used.
- Forged items are cheap to enchant: only 10 runes, trivial at 125 chantcraft skill. But disenchanting is expensive — 999 runes and 10,000 Ash — so make your first relic the one you want to keep.

| Armor | Weapon | Spell | Bow or Arrow | Skill Available | Skill Trivial |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Forge Risen | Forge Talon | Expurgation of the Forge | Forge Risen | 765 | 815 (unconfirmed) |

Forged items retain their Shadow Scale boosts and add:

- Mantle: 15% Cripple and Crippling Resist
- Shield: 15% Cripple and Crippling Resist, 10% Damage Mitigation
- Heal Spell: 15% Cripple and Crippling Resist, 25% Damage Mitigation

Cripple effect (per RWK forum): for one round of a fight all of the target's stats drop 80%, including attack and heal ratings, and the innate ruby/sapphire/emerald/void resists of armors are halved. A lesser side effect permanently reduces stats by 1,000 each — always in Inquest, and outside Inquest only if the target is above the hard cap and was active that hour.
