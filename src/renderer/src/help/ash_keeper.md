# Ash Keeper

## Overview

The Ash Keeper trades your accumulated ash for various upgrade items. He is located at **150,Sur,150** (the center of Surface).

To buy a specific item, hand the Keeper the exact amount of gold listed for that item. Giving the **wrong** gold amount gets you a **different** item than the one you wanted, so pay close attention to what he says.

Start the conversation by sending: /m Ash Keeper: hello. Progress the dialog by messaging back the bracketed keyword from each of his replies. In game the chat shows newest messages at the top, so the conversation reads bottom-to-top on screen.

## Ashen Rock Bonus

- If you carry an **Ashen Rock**, any upgrade that costs 50,000 or 100,000 ash cracks the Rock and pays out a bonus.
- The bonus type follows your poker payout setting (actions menu): payout set to **ash** gives ash; payout set to **runes** gives **Treasure Elements** (EEoGT).
- The exact ash amount or EEoGT count varies randomly within a range determined by the Rock's color: white, grey, or black.

| Skill Potencifier | 100,000 | 4 gold |

## Dialog Walkthrough

Each row shows what you message the Keeper and the gist of his reply. The next keyword to send appears in his reply.

| You send | Keeper's reply |
|---|---|
| hello | Asks for your name |
| (your name) | Greets you; tells you to go away unless you have a ton of ash |
| ash | He keeps ash while his brother collects it; asks if you understand the difference |
| yes | Wrong answer — he insists you do not understand the difference |
| difference | Demands you actually answer whether you understand the difference |
| no | Correct — you may be stupid enough to deal with |
| stupid | Offers the deal: 20k ash for an Ocean Bubble, which is pretty |
| pretty | Offers a Scepter of Annulment for 50k ash; asks if you have that much, or a 50k Ash element |
| 50k | Hand him a 50k Ash element and he converts it to 50,000 ash free of charge |
| much | For 100k ash: Beast Bane or Skill Potencifier; with a backpack, 50k ash gets a Goblin Hide |
| well | Prices: 1 gold for the Ocean Bubble, 2 for the Scepter of Annulment, 3 for the Beast Bane, 4 for the Skill Potencifier, 20 for the Goblin Hide — or ask for something else |
| else | For 50k ash each: 10 gold for Fyre Rock, 11 for Platinum Ore, 12 for Templar |
| templar | If you are already a Templar (or hold the Mask of Enshroudment) he renews it for 13 gold and 5,000 ash |
